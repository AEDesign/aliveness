Coupon House Rules:

  1. Players will obtain a coupon in exchange for a monetary (in example $5.00 donation) or physical item (food stuffs, clothing, etc). The dollar value or item will be announced before the date of the event. It will also be published via social media outlets. The coupon will be valid for one sheet of Regular Bingo paper.

  2. Coupons are valid on the night of the event only.

  3. Coupons must be redeemed with the Caller or Seller that night.

  4. One Coupon allowed per person, per event night.

  5. Coupons are only valid for play for Regular Bingo games 1 through 9. Coupons are not valid for the Progressive game 10.

  6. All other House Rules apply.
